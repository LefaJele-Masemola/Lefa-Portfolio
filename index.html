About Me

Hello I'm Lefa!
I’m a poet who codes and a coder who writes—stitching together logic and lyricism until they blur. My mind thrives in the liminal: the unsettling pause in a horror film, the quiet before a verse clicks into place, the moment an AR interface resurrects a dying language. I built a web portal for the whispers of the world—the overlooked, the almost-lost—because tech should preserve, not just progress.


Now, I’m teaching machines the poetry of hands, crafting a sign language translator to turn silence into connection. I hoard ideas like vintage horror VHS tapes—carefully, with the certainty that even the strangest fragments have value.

This portfolio is my cabinet of curiosities: some projects pulse like neon, others hum like a midnight radio playing forgotten songs. Take a look. Stay awhile. (Just don’t blame me if the code feels haunted.) Step inside.
